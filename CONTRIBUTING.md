# Contributing to Hop

Thanks for wanting to contribute. Here is how it works.

## Where to open a PR

This repository is one component of Hop. Open your pull request **here**. It is
discussed here in the open, then imported into the Hop source of truth for review
and merge, and the merged result flows back out to this repository automatically.
So your PR is the public conversation; the merge of record happens upstream and
returns here.

## Contributor License Agreement

Before your first contribution is merged you sign the [Contributor License
Agreement](./CLA.md). A bot comments on your PR with a one-line way to sign; it is a
one-time step that applies to your future contributions. The CLA lets Hop Mesh, LLC
offer Hop both as a public source-available project and under commercial licenses,
which is what funds the work.

## Before you push

Run the component's own checks (see the README's build and test section) and keep
the change focused. Small, well-described PRs land fastest.

## Security

Do not open a public issue for a security or privacy problem. See
[SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
